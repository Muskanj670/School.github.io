Admission Process

Step 1: Application Questionnaire

In Step 1 you’ll answer a few initial questions, upload your most recent grade report, and complete a short assessment. Parents are also required to answer some questions here.

Step 2:Interview with Admissions

If you are selected for an initial interview, we’ll schedule a one-hour Skype call with you and your parents to get to know you a little better and discuss your educational and personal goals.

Step 3:Full Application + Admissions Testing

If you advance after the initial interview, you will be required to complete the full online application and two assessments.

  • This includes:
  • * Additional student questions and long essay
  • * Additional parent questions
  • * Final grade reports for the past two complete academic school years
  • * Recommendation forms from your school counselor and English teacher
  • * Academic progress assessment
  • * Values assessment
  • * We will review your completed application carefully to evaluate your potential for success in a demanding overseas program.

Step 4:Faculty Interviews

If you are selected for final interviews, we’ll invite you and your parents to meet with members of the TGS faculty and school leaders via Skype. This will allow you to discuss your potential TGS learning plan and options for your specific courses.

After the interviews are completed, the TGS Admissions Committee will notify you of the final decision on your candidacy.
